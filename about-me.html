Hi There! My name is Kenny and welcome to my learning journal.

After high school, I was still unsure on which career path I wanted to pursue. At first, I went to college for a bachelor's degree in finance, and quickly realized college was not for me and decided to chase a career in web development.

Why I chose web development

After a few years of running a reselling business, it showed me how impactful a website can be for resellers. This opened my eyes to the benefits of learning how to code, along with the fact that, technology has been an evolving industry for the past few decades with new creations coming out every year.

With little to no interest in college, I knew getting a degree was completely out the window, so I thought it was best for me to invest my time in developing a skill that I can turn into a career. Besides technology, I could have taken the union route, but I didn't want to work a hard-labor job that tore my body down and possibly could have seriously injured myself.

How I plan to use my skills

After gaining all the skills I need to build websites, I plan on searching for jobs to keep me busy during the day and earn a stable income. With my time outside of work, I would be researching how I can use my skills to create side-hustles or even my own business.
